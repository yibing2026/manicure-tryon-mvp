# Operations Strategy Rules V1

## Core Positioning
- Prioritize premium glossy styles for attention-grabbing campaigns.
- Use bridal/elegant clusters for trust-building and high-value conversions.
- Use sweet/daily clusters for volume, student reach, and low-threshold offers.

## Persona Rules
- minimalist: Prioritize minimal styles with white tones for minimalist.
- young-professional: Prioritize elegant, sweet styles with gray, green, nude tones for young-professional.
- fashion-lover: Prioritize cool-girl, luxury styles with black, blue, nude tones for fashion-lover.
- student: Prioritize daily, sweet styles with nude, pink, red tones for student.
- luxury-seeker: Prioritize luxury styles with nude tones for luxury-seeker.
- trend-follower: Prioritize cool-girl, sweet styles with black, blue, pink tones for trend-follower.
- bridal-user: Prioritize elegant, luxury styles with nude, white tones for bridal-user.

## Occasion Rules
- commute: Build commute campaigns around elegant, minimal styles and highlight gray, white palettes.
- dating: Build dating campaigns around elegant, sweet styles and highlight green, nude, pink palettes.
- party: Build party campaigns around cool-girl, luxury styles and highlight black, blue, nude palettes.
- daily: Build daily campaigns around cool-girl, daily styles and highlight blue, nude palettes.
- wedding: Build wedding campaigns around elegant, luxury styles and highlight nude, pink, white palettes.
- festival: Build festival campaigns around sweet styles and highlight nude, pink palettes.

## Trend Clusters
- premium_party_gloss: Use for high-attention homepage slots and night-out campaign creatives.
- bridal_elegant_clean: Package as bridal / event bundles and use polished trust-building copy.
- cute_youth_festival: Use for campus, holiday, and low-threshold conversion campaigns.

## Inventory Actions
- inventory_balance: Increase short daily-safe inventory exposure for broader conversion coverage.
- premium_focus: Create premium merchandising slots and bundle copy for party and bridal users.
- daily_gap: Promote daily-safe styles aggressively to office and minimalist audiences to fill the practical-demand gap.
